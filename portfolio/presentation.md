# Sentinel AI - Presentation Pitch

## The Problem
Public safety and private security forces rely on outdated, human-monitored CCTV networks. Operators suffer from fatigue, missing critical events. The result? Delayed response times to violent crimes and suspicious activities.

## The Solution
**Sentinel AI**: An enterprise-grade, real-time video analytics platform that transforms passive cameras into active threat-detection agents.

## Core Capabilities
1. **Instant Weapon Detection**: Recognizes firearms and bladed weapons in milliseconds.
2. **Violence & Fight Recognition**: Analyzes skeletal movement to detect physical altercations.
3. **Facial Recognition**: Automatically matches suspects against a secure, encrypted criminal database.
4. **Automated Dispatch**: Sends immediate, high-priority alerts to security personnel via Telegram and Email.

## Technical Superiority
- **Scalable Architecture**: Built with FastAPI and a modern React 19 Frontend.
- **Hardware Acceleration**: Deep learning inference powered by PyTorch and ONNXRuntime, supporting both CPU fallbacks and NVIDIA GPU acceleration.
- **Enterprise UI**: Dark-themed, glassmorphism dashboard designed for extended surveillance operations.

## Market Potential
Applicable for schools, enterprise campuses, retail loss prevention, and municipal law enforcement.
